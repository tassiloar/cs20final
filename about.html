About Us

Welcome to the Tufts University Book Club Finder! Our mission is to help you connect with fellow readers on campus, discover new genres, and engage in vibrant reading discussions. Whether you're into non-fiction, poetry, mystery, romance, or academic literature, there's a book club for everyone at Tufts University.

At Tufts, we believe in fostering a community that values curiosity, open dialogue, and intellectual exploration beyond the classroom. Our platform makes it easy to connect with others who share a love for reading, allowing students, faculty, and staff to come together and explore diverse perspectives through books. Whether you're looking to dive deep into classic literature or simply unwind with light fiction, our clubs cover a wide range of interests.

We understand how challenging it can be to make time for hobbies amid a busy academic schedule. That's why we've designed this platform to streamline the process of finding a club that aligns with both your interests and availability. With flexible meeting times and a wide selection of genres, we hope to make reading more accessible and enjoyable for the entire Tufts community.

Our book clubs provide a supportive and engaging environment where members can exchange ideas, recommend favorite reads, and build lasting friendships. Joining a book club is not only an opportunity to expand your literary horizons, but it's also a way to make connections with people who might become lifelong friends.

So, come join us, share your love for reading, and create meaningful connections here at Tufts University. Discover the transformative power of books, one club meeting at a time!
